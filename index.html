A Great Place To Hang Out

Creedmoor Market & Wine Shop is a neighborhood wine bar and market with a terrific wine selection. We pride ourselves with providing our patrons with great service and, as our slogan states, "A great place to hang out." Through countless renovations since being established as a convenience store in ______, we've seen our shop transform into what it is today: a wine and beer shop where locals can hang out. Military Wall of Honor

We honor our military veterans and their service to this country, and want to show our respect to those who serve in our own community. Our Military Wall of Honor is a tribute to those brave men and women in uniform.

All service members are welcome to put their signature on the wall, along with what branch you belonged to. Come by and talk to us if you would like to have your framed picture on the wall for the community to see!
